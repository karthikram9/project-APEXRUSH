VitalScan health risk web app.
STACK: React+Vite+Tailwind, FastAPI, PostgreSQL, Redis, Scikit-learn, Recharts.
INPUTS: age,sex,height,weight,waist_cm,physical_activity,sleep_hours,
stress_level,family_history_heart,smoking_status,fried_food,
chest_discomfort,salt_intake,family_history_diab,sugar_intake,
water_intake,excessive_thirst.
FEATURES:
1.Single form→3 simultaneous predictions:
-Heart risk (GradientBoosting .pkl)
-Diabetes risk (GradientBoosting .pkl)
-Obesity risk (WHO BMI+WHR formula)
2.Dashboard:3 color-coded scores(green/yellow/red)+top risk factors.
3.Live sliders→instant score updates via Redis.
4.Priority-ranked preventive action plan.
DISCLAIMER every page:"Educational only.Not medical diagnosis.
Consult a doctor."
